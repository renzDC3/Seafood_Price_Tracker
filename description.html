Seafood Price Tracker is an interactive website whose sole purpose is to provide Filipino internet users a way to determine the current prices of their beloved seafood delicacies from the comfort of their own home. Our website updates the prices of each fish and mollusk on a daily basis, which makes sure that every person about to go to the supermarket to buy seafood for their beloved families, or themselves, are carrying right amount of money for the purchasing of these delicious sea creatures. Our website is free to be accessed by everyone because we want every Filipino with a phone and internet connection to be able to use our services. Making this website required time and effort to make.
